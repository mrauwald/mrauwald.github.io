MARISA RAUWALD

A Durham-based videographer and photographer who enjoys traveling, sports, music, and food.

My name is Marisa Rauwald and I am a junior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in broadcast & electronic journalism and media & technology studies & production.

When I was younger, I used to make music videos for fun. I would make my friends dress up and act in character while I shot, edited, and directed. I guess you could say I got pretty into it. At the time, I never really thought too much about this interest; who wouldn't want to make music videos with their friends?

Looking back, I realize that the enjoyment I received from making these videos was unparalleled. Through my various video production experiences in college, my hobby of making music videos has turned into a passion for videography.
