What are alpacas?

The alpaca is a species of South American camelid.

What Do I Eat?

Alpacas mainly eat pasture grass, hay, or silage but some may also need supplemental energy and protein foods and they will also normally try to chew on almost anything.

Where To Find Me?

Alpacas are native to Peru; it currently has the largest population of alpacas in the world.

Alpacas can have many different fur coats.

Alpacas can be found in mountainous regions.
